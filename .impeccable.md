## Design Context

### Users
Ebisu is for a broad range of everyday people who want to budget simply without being trapped between oversimplified apps and overpriced “premium” tools. They need enough depth to shape a budget around their real household, but not so much complexity that budgeting becomes intimidating or exhausting.

### Brand Personality
Calm, warm, reassuring. Ebisu should make a scary and emotionally uncomfortable subject feel softer, easier, and more approachable without becoming childish or unserious.

### Aesthetic Direction
A refined blend of guided ritual and practical setup flow: gentle, domestic, ceremonial in tone, but still clear and useful. The product should feel meaningfully more human than a generic finance dashboard while remaining simple enough for ordinary users to trust.

### Design Principles
1. Make depth feel approachable: customization should unfold gradually instead of arriving as complexity all at once.
2. Reduce financial anxiety: copy, pacing, and visuals should make the user feel guided rather than judged.
3. Preserve practical clarity beneath the atmosphere: Ebisu’s voice should soften the experience, not obscure what the user is doing.
4. Prefer progressive disclosure over premature completeness: reveal the next layer only when the user is ready for it.
5. Distinguish the product through warmth and care, not through artificial limitation or expensive-feeling complexity.
